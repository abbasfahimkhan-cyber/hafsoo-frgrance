Shipping Policy

Order Processing

We believe in delivering excellence. Every order is carefully packed to ensure the safety of our premium glass bottles.

  • Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days.
  • Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day.
  • You will receive an email/SMS confirmation once your order is processed.

Delivery Time

We partner with reliable courier services to ensure your signature scent reaches you in perfect condition.

  • Standard Delivery: 3–5 business days nationwide.
  • Remote areas may take an additional 2-3 business days.
  • Please note that delivery times may be extended during peak seasons or sales events.

Shipping Charges

Our shipping rates are designed to be fair and transparent:

  • Free Shipping: On all orders above Rs. 5000.
  • Standard Rate: A flat fee of Rs. 250 applies to orders below Rs. 5000.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) services are available across Pakistan.

Order Tracking Information

Stay updated on your journey to luxury. Once your order is dispatched, you will receive:

  • A tracking number via SMS and Email.
  • A direct link to our courier partner's tracking portal.
  • Real-time status updates from dispatch to delivery.

If you haven't received your tracking details after 3 days, please contact our Customer Care team.
