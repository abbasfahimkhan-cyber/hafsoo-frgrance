Privacy Policy

Information Collection

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. We collect information through the following methods:

  • Personal details provided by you (Name, Shipping Address, Phone Number, Email).
  • Technical data (IP Address, Browser Type) as you navigate our website.
  • Transaction history related to your orders.

Use of Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide a better shopping experience:

  • To process and deliver your orders efficiently.
  • To send order confirmations and tracking updates.
  • To personalize your experience and show relevant perfume recommendations.
  • To improve our website's functionality and security.

Data Protection

We implement strict security measures to ensure your data stays safe:

  • Use of SSL encryption for all data transfers.
  • Secure databases with limited access to authorized personnel.
  • We never sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies Usage

Our website uses cookies to enhance your journey. Cookies help us:

  • Remember items in your shopping cart.
  • Understand your preferences based on previous site activity.
  • Compile aggregate data about site traffic to offer better site experiences.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but some parts of our website may not function correctly as a result.
