Refund Policy

Eligibility for Refund

We want you to love your purchase. If you are not entirely satisfied, we're here to help.

  • The product must be unused and in its original premium packaging.
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of delivery.
  • A valid proof of purchase (Order Confirmation email) is required.

Non-Refundable Items

Due to health and hygiene standards, certain items are exempt from being returned:

  • Opened or used perfume and attar bottles.
  • Items purchased during clearance or special promotional sales.
  • Gift cards and loyalty vouchers.

Refund Process

Once we receive your request, our team will guide you through the next steps:

  • Contact our Customer Care team to initiate the process.
  • The returned product must pass a quality inspection.
  • Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Shipping Cost Responsibility

  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable.
  • Customers are responsible for paying their own shipping costs for returning an item.
  • If the product is proven to be defective or damaged, we will cover the return shipping costs.

Damaged or Wrong Product

In the rare event that you receive a damaged or incorrect item, we take full responsibility.

  • The issue must be reported within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Please provide clear photos of the damaged bottle or packaging.
  • We will provide a replacement or a full refund after verification.
